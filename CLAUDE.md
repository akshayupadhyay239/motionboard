@AGENTS.md

## Design Context

### Users
Broad creative audience: freelance motion designers, AI video creators, filmmakers, video editors, hobbyists. Job to be done: find a contest I can actually win, enter it, get paid. Browsed opportunistically during creative sessions.

### Brand Personality
**3 words: urgent, curated, kinetic**

Emotional goal: *exciting discovery* — the feeling of finding money on the table that most people don't know about. Not a job board. Not a feed. A bulletin board run by someone with taste.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Light theme — warm cream + deep ink. Existing palette (#F4F3EF cream, #0D0D0D ink, #1C1C8E navy accent) is correct, needs refinement.
- Replace Inter + Playfair Display — both are reflex defaults. Go for something more kinetic.
- Navy accent is underused — lean into it for deadlines, prize callouts, urgency signals.
- Editorial asymmetry: left-aligned, varied scale. Prize amounts are the visual anchor.

### Anti-References
Generic SaaS, job boards (LinkedIn/Indeed), AI tool landing pages (dark + glowing gradients).

### Design Principles
1. **Money first** — Prize amounts visually dominate every card and listing.
2. **Urgency feels exciting, not anxious** — Deadlines create energy, not dread.
3. **Curated, not crawled** — Intentional spacing, deliberate typography, no algorithmic density.
4. **Kinetic DNA** — Purposeful motion: card hovers, subtle transitions, stagger on load.
5. **No corporate DNA** — Made by a creator for creators.
